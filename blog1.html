Top 10 Games of 2017


More than 400 games were released during 2017. But only a few games achieved success. Totally 2017 is not a great year for gaming but there are some successful and good games were released. We listed 10 games which are successful and people liked it.

10. Resident Evil 7: BIOHAZARD.

Resident Evil 7: BIOHAZARD is a survival horror first-person shooter game which is developed and published by Capcom. It was released worldwide on January 24, 2017 for PC,PS4,Xbox One.This game has only single player mode and player controls the protagonist, Ethan from a first-person perspective. The story follows protagonist as he searches for his wife which leads him to Baker Family. There are lot of puzzles in the game and gameplay is completely different from previous resident evil games. It is the first full-length game to be developed in RE Engine.

9. Cuphead

Cuphead is a run and gun game which is an indie game developed and published by StudioMDHR. It was released on September 29, 2017 for PC and Xbox One. This game has single player and two players local co-op mode. It has role-playing elements and continuous boss fights. After the completion of a level player will be ranked with grade based on their performance in that level. Cuphead sold over one million copies worldwide in the first two weeks of release.

8. Hellblade :Senua’s Sacrifice

Hellblade is an action-adventure, hack and slash game developed and published by Ninja Theory. It was released worldwide on August 8, 2017 for PC and PS4. This game has only single-player mode. It is inspired by Norse Mythology and Celtic culture. Game is set in the 8th century and Player controls the protagonist Senua, a pict warrior who has to rescue the dead soul of her dead lover from Hela. Voice acting is an integral part of this game. This game was a commercial success and received very positive reviews.

7. Nier:Automata

Nier: Automata is an action-adventure role-playing game which is developed by PlatinumGames and published by Square Enix. It was released worldwide on March 17, 2017 for PC and PS4. This game has only single-player mode. It is a sequel to Nier game which was released in 2010. It combines roleplaying elements with action-based combat and mixed genre gameplay similar to Nier. Player takes the role of combat androids from yorha units across an open world environment. Game received very positive reviews from both critics and players.

6. Horizon Zero Dawn

Horizon Zero Dawn is an action role-playing game which is developed by Guerrilla Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It was released on 28 February, 2017 as a PS4 exclusive. This game has only single-player mode. Player takes control of a hunter and an archer Aloy. Game is set in an open world overrun by robots. This game was built using Decima game engine. It received very positive reviews and was praised for its open world,story,visual,combat. It was sold more than three million copies and later they added an expansion Frozen Wilds on November 7, 2017.

5. Wolfenstein 2: The New Colossus

Wolfenstein 2: The New Colossus is an action-adventure first-person shooter game which is developed by Machine Games and published by Bethesda Softworks. It was released worldwide on October 27, 2017 for PC,PS4,Xbox One and Nintendo Switch. It is the eighth main entry in the Wolfenstein franchise. This game is set in alternate history where Nazis won the second world war. The story follows William “B.J.” Blazkowickz and his effort to fight against Nazi regime. The game received positive response and reviews.

4. Divinity :Original Sin 2

Divinity: Original Sin 2 is a role-playing game which is developed and published by Larian Studios. It was released worldwide on September 14, 2017 for PC. It is a sequel to the Divinity :Original sin which was released in 2014. It has both single player and multiplayer mode. Players can play solo with only one character in the party or with up to three players. It received very positive reviews from critics and considering it to be one of the best roleplaying games of all time. And also it sold over one million copies in two months.

3. Persona 5

Persona 5 is a role-playing game which is developed and published by Atlus. It was released worldwide on April 4, 2017 for PS3 and PS4. It is the sixth installment in Persona series. Game was first released in Japan and later it was released worldwide. Game takes place in Tokyo and player takes control of high school student. Persona 5 has been called one of the greatest role-playing game of all time by critics.

2. PlayerUnkown’s Battlegrounds

PUBG is a multiplayer online battle royale game which is developed and published by PUBG corporation (subsidiary of BlueHole).It was inspired by movie BattleRoyale which was released in 2010. The game was first released for PC as an early access in March 2017. And later it was released on 20 December,2017 for PC and 12 December 2017 for Xbox One. Steam version has sold over twenty-four million copies and held a peak concurrent player of nearly three million. PUBG is a PvP action game in which up to hundred players fight in a battle royale and last man standing will win the match. Players can enter match solo, or with a small team up to four players.

1. The Legend Of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The Legend Of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an action adventure game which is developed and published by Nintendo. It was released worldwide on March 3, 2017 for Nintendo Switch and Wii U. They introduced new mechanics and designs including an open-world environment to the game and it took almost five years for development. Game sold more than four million copies. It received universal acclaim from critics and won Game of the Year award.
